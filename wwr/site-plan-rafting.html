Overview

Purpose

Because many costumers and potential customers rely on online information to make decisions, we have found it necessary to introduce our the presence of our company to the world wide web. In doing this we hope to provide answers to all of our costumers information so they can feel informed enough to comfotably try our services.

Audience

Our audience is customers of our white water rafting business as well as potential customers and interested parties. We hope to provide information about our services to all in a appealing way. Users mainly between the ages of 16-35 will need to be able to access our website on a mobile device or computer. Our target customers are those ages from 16-35 who like to be active and spend time outdoors.
